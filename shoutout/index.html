FAQs

How does Shoutout24 work?

We are unique as we providing best services and care to our women and children that face abuse. Our work is rooted in the believe that change can only be said to be effective when women lives are directly improved through our intervention. We believe that these no change thus constantly seek new and creative approaches in our programming to achieve the change we seek

Where can I use Shoutout24?

Shoutout24 can be used anywhere. You can download it from the App Store and Playstore.

Is Shoutout24 only for women and children?

Although Shoutout24 was created to address the difficulties women go through on a daily basis, it is not limited to women only. Anyone can join and use the app.

Do I need to share my location on at all times?

No, Shoudout 24 only tracks your location if you hold the emergency button or send your location. It will send your live location for 5 hours or until you stop sharing your location on the menu.
